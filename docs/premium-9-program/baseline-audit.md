# Premium 9 baseline audit

## Evidence and fixed policy

The baseline is starting SHA `2972829289c9c7f71d2aa533eca1db8b1119140c`, rendered through the production preview at `http://127.0.0.1:4322`. The accepted evidence set contains 522 valid screenshots: five top viewports and four deterministic chapter/ending captures for each of 58 routes. Malformed full-page captures and screenshots containing the Astro development toolbar were rejected.

The scoring scale and the 50 global dimensions below were fixed before runtime edits. A 9 means distinctive, highly intentional, exceptionally polished, and free of an obvious defect under close review. Passing technical checks is necessary but does not increase a visual score by itself. Where reviewers disagree materially, the lowest credible score governs.
