# Content Boundaries

Date: 2026-07-06

## Ownership Model

Manish Sharma Lab owns the personal expert layer:

- Public identity and profile context
- Industrial AI thesis
- Decision frameworks
- AI-agent guidance
- Public-safe tools
- Source maps
- Lab notes
- Glossary pages
- Evidence interpretation

Exafuse owns the company and commercial layer:

- Commercial additive manufacturing services
- RFQs and contact path
- Company case studies
- Company quality pages
- Technology and production capability claims
- Customer/project delivery context

The two sites should support each other through clean canonical links, not duplicate service content.

## Confidentiality Rules

Do not publish:

- Confidential Exafuse information
- Customer names or customer-specific project data unless already public and approved
- Private material, machine, inspection, pricing, or capability data
- Unannounced projects
- Employer-confidential or commercially sensitive ideas
- Private project drafts hidden in public routes or source files

## Claim Rules

Do not claim:

- AI tools provide engineering approval
- Process monitoring proves final part quality
- Melt-pool monitoring is a quality certificate
- A framework certifies material properties
- A tool guarantees repair feasibility
- The personal site is an Exafuse service page

Use:

- `Preliminary decision-support only. Final feasibility depends on base material, geometry, service conditions, inspection requirements, and expert review.`
- `Public profile materials describe...`
- `Current public focus includes...`
- `Selected public work includes...`
- `Profile details should be verified against LinkedIn/CV where required.`

## Public Proof Rules

Public Exafuse pages may be used as proof context when linked carefully. The personal site may explain why a case matters for monitoring, repairability, RFQ structure, evidence ladders, or decision systems.

The personal site should not reproduce company case details beyond public-safe summary language.

## AI And Engineering Boundary

AI is framed as:

- Structured thinking support
- Missing-information detection
- RFQ preparation support
- Monitoring interpretation support
- Risk flagging
- Evidence routing
- Decision support

AI is not framed as:

- Final approval
- Certification
- Safety-critical acceptance
- Material qualification
- Quality guarantee
- Replacement for expert review
