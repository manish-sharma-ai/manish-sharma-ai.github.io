## LinkedIn Launch Post - Version A

I published LMD Decision Brief v1.0, a public format for making Laser Metal Deposition and Directed Energy Deposition questions easier to review.

The problem is simple: early LMD/DED requests often mix known facts, missing information, risk flags, process signals, and quality assumptions in one paragraph. That makes expert review harder than it needs to be.

The brief separates:

- known facts
- critical missing information
- useful optional context
- risk flags
- evidence needed
- preliminary route
- expert-review next step

It runs in the browser, has no backend, and does not send or store inputs. It is decision support only. It is not approval, certification, release evidence, or a quality guarantee.

Start here:
https://manishsharma.dev/brief-standard/
