# Site narrative v2

## Position

- Category: Industrial AI & Decision Systems.
- Promise: AI for industrial decisions that need evidence, not just predictions.
- Method: Sense → Model → Decide → Verify.
- Current public proving ground: LMD/DED at Exafuse.
- Portable artifact: LMD Decision Brief v1.0.

## Homepage sequence

1. Positioning hero and one dominant product action.
2. Compact public proof: a real industrial case, working product, and authored evidence boundary.
3. LMD Decision Cockpit preview.
4. Concise method.
5. Asymmetric selected work: proof lead plus two supporting artifacts.
6. First-person mission and personal collaboration route.
7. Explicit personal-platform / Exafuse-commercial boundary.

This order keeps product and proof ahead of taxonomy. The homepage melt-pool image remains homepage-specific; other pages can use warm matte surfaces, copper evidence routes, and real case imagery only where it carries a source-backed narrative purpose.

## Visual roles

- Copper: human decision and professional action.
- Cyan: active instrument state and measured signal only.
- Amber: heat, risk, uncertainty, and missing evidence.
- Mineral white: primary authored thought.
- Muted steel: supporting context.
- Warm matte dark surfaces: editorial canvas.

## Navigation

The primary path remains Home, Proof, Method, Notes, and About. Reference is a compact chooser for Tools, Frameworks, Sources, Brief Standard, and All Resources. The complete route inventory remains available through All Resources, footer, site map, and command search.
